Sucafina is one of the world's leading sustainable coffee traders, operating across origination, processing, logistics, and financing, spanning Kenya, Ethiopia, and global markets.

Lua AI proposes to begin with a structured **Audit & Prototype Engagement**: a focused discovery and build sprint that maps your highest-value workflows, develops working AI agent prototypes, and gives your team a hands-on basis for a go/no-go decision.

## Phase 1: Audit & Prototype Engagement

A focused engagement to go deep into Sucafina's operations, identify where AI agents can deliver the most value, and build working prototypes, so decisions are based on real demos, not slides.

### What's Included

**Audit & Discovery**
- Structured sessions with stakeholders across trading, operations, finance, HR, and quality
- Mapping of key workflows, systems, and integration points
- Prioritisation of use cases by value, complexity, and scalability

**Prototype & Demo Development**
- Development of 5–10 working AI agent prototypes across prioritised use cases
- Each prototype is a functional demo, not a mockup, that your team can interact with
- If the audit uncovers additional high-value use cases, we scope and build those within this engagement at no extra cost

**Go / No-Go Review**
- Presentation of all prototypes to the Sucafina leadership team
- Honest assessment of what's ready to scale, what needs refinement, and what to prioritise
- Roadmap and pricing for the next phase shared at this stage
